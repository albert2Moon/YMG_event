Discover the story behind YMG where creativity and excitment converge. Learn about our mission, team, and the driving force behind unforgettable experiences.

Our Story

YMG was started by a small group of youths intending to bring all youth together and now it's growing bigger and bigger each and every year that passes by and soon we shall not be able to hold it indoors but open space.

Our Mission

The mission for the event is to unite Youth from different churches and communities and most importantly to win souls to the Kingdom of God throughful praises and activities.

Our Activities

  • Praise and Worship
  • Fashion Show
  • Dance and Drama
  • Special Presentations
  • Testimonies
  • Prayer
